### Problem 1 — Related Rates
A ladder 5 m long leans against a wall. Its base slides away at 0.5 m/s. How fast is the top sliding down when the base is 3 m from the wall?

**Solution:**
- x² + y² = 25, differentiate: 2x(dx/dt) + 2y(dy/dt) = 0
- When x = 3: y = √(25−9) = 4
- dy/dt = −x(dx/dt)/y = −3(0.5)/4 = **−0.375 m/s** (sliding down)

### Problem 2 — Integration by Parts
Evaluate ∫ x²eˣ dx

**Solution:**
- Apply integration by parts twice: ∫u dv = uv − ∫v du
- Round 1: u = x², dv = eˣdx → du = 2x dx, v = eˣ
  → x²eˣ − ∫2xeˣ dx
- Round 2: u = 2x, dv = eˣdx → du = 2dx, v = eˣ
  → x²eˣ − [2xeˣ − ∫2eˣ dx]
- = x²eˣ − 2xeˣ + 2eˣ + C = **eˣ(x² − 2x + 2) + C**
