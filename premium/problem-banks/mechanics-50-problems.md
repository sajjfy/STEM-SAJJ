### Problem 1 — Projectile Motion
A ball is launched at 30° above horizontal with initial speed 20 m/s from a cliff 45 m high. Find: (a) time of flight, (b) horizontal range, (c) speed at impact.

**Solution:**
- Decompose: v₀ₓ = 20cos30° = 17.32 m/s, v₀ᵧ = 20sin30° = 10 m/s
- Vertical: −45 = 10t − ½(9.81)t² → 4.905t² − 10t − 45 = 0
- t = (10 ± √(100 + 4×4.905×45)) / (2×4.905) = (10 + 30.4) / 9.81 = **4.12 s**
- Range: x = 17.32 × 4.12 = **71.4 m**
- vₓ = 17.32 m/s, vᵧ = 10 − 9.81×4.12 = −30.4 m/s
- Speed = √(17.32² + 30.4²) = **34.9 m/s**

### Problem 2 — Rotational Inertia
A solid cylinder (m = 5 kg, r = 0.2 m) rolls without slipping down a 15° incline of length 3 m from rest. Find its speed at the bottom.

**Solution:**
- Energy conservation: mgh = ½mv² + ½Iω²
- For solid cylinder: I = ½mr², ω = v/r
- mgh = ½mv² + ½(½mr²)(v/r)² = ½mv² + ¼mv² = ¾mv²
- v² = (4/3)gh = (4/3)(9.81)(3sin15°) = (4/3)(9.81)(0.776) = 10.16
- **v = 3.19 m/s**

### Problem 3 — Momentum & Impulse
A 0.15 kg cricket ball travelling at 35 m/s is struck by a bat and rebounds at 45 m/s in the opposite direction. Contact time is 1.2 ms. Find: (a) impulse, (b) average force.

**Solution:**
- Taking initial direction as positive
- Δp = m(v_f − v_i) = 0.15(−45 − 35) = 0.15(−80) = **−12 N·s**
- |Impulse| = **12 N·s**
- F_avg = Δp/Δt = 12 / (1.2×10⁻³) = **10,000 N**
