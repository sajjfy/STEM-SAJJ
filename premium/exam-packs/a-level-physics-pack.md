## Preview — Mechanics Q1

**[6 marks]** A car of mass 1200 kg travelling at 25 m/s brakes to a stop over 40 m. Calculate:
(a) the deceleration [2]
(b) the braking force [2]
(c) the kinetic energy dissipated as heat [2]

**Mark Scheme:**
- (a) v² = u² + 2as → 0 = 625 + 2a(40) → a = **−7.81 m/s²** ✓✓
- (b) F = ma = 1200 × 7.81 = **9375 N** ✓✓
- (c) ΔKE = ½mu² = ½(1200)(625) = **375,000 J = 375 kJ** ✓✓

**Common mistakes:**
- Forgetting v² = 0 at rest (using v = 0 in wrong equation)
- Not converting to correct sig figs for the paper (3 s.f. required)
